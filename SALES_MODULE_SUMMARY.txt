================================================================================
                            HOW TO USE
================================================================================

1. LOGIN
   - Username: admin
   - Password: admin123

2. CREATE A SALE
   - Navigate to Sales → New Sale
   - Select customer (optional)
   - Choose payment method
   - Add medicines from dropdown
   - Adjust quantities
   - Add tax/discount if needed
   - Click "Complete Sale"

3. VIEW SALES
   - Go to Sales Management
   - Browse all transactions
   - Click "View" for details
   - Click "Print" for invoice

4. PRINT INVOICE
   - Opens in new window
   - Click "Print Invoice" button
   - Or use Ctrl+P
